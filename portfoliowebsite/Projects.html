A birth registration form is vital for legally documenting a person's birth, establishing their identity, and ensuring their rights to citizenship.
It provides essential data for public health and demographic statistics,aids in accessing government services and benefits,
and helps protect against identity fraud. Accurate birth registration is fundamental for individual and societal well-being.

A death registration form is crucial for legally documenting a person's death, which is necessary for settling estates, claiming life insurance, and terminating social security benefits.
It provides essential data for public health statistics, helping to inform healthcare policies and resource allocation.
Accurate death registration also aids in preventing identity fraud and ensuring proper legal and administrative processes are followed.
